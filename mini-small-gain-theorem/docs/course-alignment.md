# Course Alignment — Small Gain Theorem

Nine-school curriculum mapping for robust control / small gain theorem topics.

## MIT
- Course: 6.241 (Dynamic Systems & Control), 6.821 (Underactuated Robotics)
- Alignment: L2 gain, passivity, small gain theorem (§9.3 in 6.241)
- Covered: H∞ norm, BRL, passivity index, scattering transform

## Stanford
- Course: EE363 (Linear Dynamical Systems), AA278 (Advanced Control)
- Alignment: Robust stability via SGT, mu analysis, D-K iteration
- Covered: Structured uncertainty, mu bounds, D-scaling

## Berkeley
- Course: ME C232 (Advanced Control Systems II)
- Alignment: Robust control with H∞ and mu synthesis (Horowitz)
- Covered: H∞ bisection, ARE, BRL

## CMU
- Course: 24-771 (Linear Control Systems), 24-787 (Robust Control)
- Alignment: Frequency-domain robust control, gap metric
- Covered: Gap/nu-gap metric, robust stability margin

## Princeton
- Course: MAE 546 (Optimal Control and Estimation)
- Alignment: Riccati equations, H2/H∞ control, BRL
- Covered: CARE solver, H2/H∞ norms, Lyapunov equations

## Caltech
- Course: CDS 110 (Introduction to Control Theory), CDS 212 (Robust Control)
- Alignment: Small gain, passivity, IQC framework (Doyle, Murray)
- Covered: IQC multipliers, scattering transform, passivity

## Cambridge
- Course: 4F2 (Robust and Nonlinear Control)
- Alignment: SGT, uncertainty modeling, gap metric (Glover, Smith)
- Covered: Multiplicative/additive weights, gap metric

## Oxford
- Course: B16 (Control Systems)
- Alignment: Robust stability, H∞ loop shaping, mu analysis
- Covered: H∞ norms, mu analysis, D-K iteration

## ETH
- Course: 227-0216-00L (Control Systems II)
- Alignment: Robust control, H∞ synthesis, LMI methods (Smith, D'Andrea)
- Covered: BRL, ARE, Lyapunov stability, IQC framework
